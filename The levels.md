Level 0: The Endless Archive.
  A maze of endless corridors full of shelves, chests, cabinets, barrels, and pallets of sorted detritus from the millions of extradimensional spaces that have leaked or collapsed. Strange spider-like beings roam the corridors cleaning and sorting what they come across.
  Either warehouses or libraries can be found here, as well as other things that call the halls of the Endless Archive home.
Level 1: The Canals.
  Tunnels of brick, stone, or simply carved into rock channel various liquids, usually water, to other destinations. Cisterns, bottoms of wells, sewers can be found as well as the things that live in them.
