### Level 0: The Endless Archive.

  A maze of endless corridors full of shelves, chests, cabinets, barrels, and pallets of sorted detritus from the millions of extradimensional spaces that have leaked or collapsed. Strange spider-like beings, colloquially called "The Collectors", roam the corridors cleaning and sorting what they come across.  
  
  Either warehouses or libraries can be found here, as well as other things that call the halls of the Endless Archive home.  

### Level 1: The Eternal Library
  Adjacent to the Endless Archive is the Eternal Library. While called Level 1, it technically exists as part of level 0. However, it gains its distinction as its own level as one cannot access it without going through the Endless Archive first and is distinctly its own region. The Collectors bring any and every piece of writing to this section for catalogue. Acient tomes of magic, clay tablets, slates of wood carved in runes, and hastily written notes written on napkins that end up in the Archive in any and every concievable language are brought here by the Collectors. The collectors rarely stay though, dropping off their findings into repositories. A subset of Collectors take over. They're longer and taller, walking on four of their eight legs and using their other four limbs to catalogue, sort, and shelve the various media that get dropped off. Unlike the thier common Collector bretheren, the Librarians are distinquied by their white shawls edged with ribbons of ancient writings and may have a basket for which to hold more media resting on thier mechanical gasters. They seem to be able to understand speech, but genrally remain silent and aloof while going about thier work. Whenever an outsider enters the Eternal Library, a Librarian is guaranteed to be nearby, following. Watching. Cataloging.  
  
  The Librarians aren't hostile as long as you follow the rules.  
  * NOTHING archived in the Library leaves the Library.
  * If you pull a book from a shelf, do not attempt to put it back yourself. It's better to just leave it on the floor or hand it to the Librarian following you.
  * Give a Librarian a book or other form of media from the outside while stating any information on a piece of media you're looking for. If the Librarian nods, follow it. When it stops, look around. It'll be in that area. If it doesn't nod, you're on your own.

### Level 2: The Canals.
  Tunnels of brick, stone, or simply carved into rock channel various liquids, usually water, to other destinations. Cisterns, bottoms of wells, sewers can be found as well as the things that live in them.
